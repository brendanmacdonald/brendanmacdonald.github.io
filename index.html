About me and this site

I have been a Test Analyst for 20 years. My aim for 2023 is to transition to a Data Analyst role for the seond half of my career.

To make this happen, I have spent the last year studying data analysis processes and tools in order to upskill myself to a sufficient level to take on a junior data analyst role. I divided my efforts across 7 subjects:

  1. Power BI
  2. Data Modeling
  3. DAX
  4. SQL
  5. Data Warehousing

I used multiple different online course providers that supplied video content and course materials. As can be seen from the report, the core of my studies have been course material provided by Microsoft.

I have received certifications from all of the courses that I have done, where one was provided. Additionally, I have to-date sat and passed one related exam, 'PL-900 Microsoft Power PLatform Fundamentals' and I am aiming to sit the 'PL-300: Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst' in the coming months.
