About me and this site

I have been a Test Analyst for 20 years. My aim for 2023 is to transition to a Data Analyst role for the second half of my career.

To make this happen, I have spent the last year studying data analysis processes and tools in order to upskill myself to a sufficient level to take on a junior data analyst role. I divided my study time across 7 subjects:

  1. Power BI
  2. Data Modeling
  3. DAX
  4. SQL
  5. Data Warehousing
  6. Data Analytics
  7. Power Platform

I used multiple different online course providers that supplied video content and course materials. The core of my studies have been course material provided by Microsoft and LinkedIn Learning.

I have received certifications from all of the courses that I have done, where one was provided. Additionally, I have sat and passed one related exam, 'PL-900 Microsoft Power Platform Fundamentals'. I am aiming to sit the 'PL-300: Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst' in the coming months.
